# RubiSCoT_Validation_Study_Data

This repository contains the anonymized raw survey data from a research study on AI-based grading and assessment in higher education. The dataset includes responses from academic staff and thesis evaluators regarding their grading experience, disciplinary background, use of AI technologies, perceptions of AI-supported assessment, and related evaluation practices. All personally identifiable information has been removed or anonymized. The data are provided for research transparency, replication, secondary analysis, and educational purposes.
